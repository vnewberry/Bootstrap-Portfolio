About Me

My name is Vance Newberry. I was born and raised in Paducah, Kentucky. In January of 2020 I started as a student in Vanderbilt University's Coding Bootcamp and I'm excited to begin a bright career as a software/web developer upon graduation. Before starting my education as a fullstack developer I worked as a brick and stone mason for 3 years and as an apprentice mason/laborer for 5 years prior to that. I have a Bachelors degree in mathematics from Western Kentucky which I completed in May 2015. I am confident that my unique background in mathematics and construction both will enable me to excel as a developer in many different ways.


Besides work and school I have many interests that I pursue in my free time. I enjoy most outdoor activities and consider myself an avid, but amateur, fisherman. When the weather is not so great you can find me training in the gym, reading a book, playing a video game, or trying out a new recipe.
